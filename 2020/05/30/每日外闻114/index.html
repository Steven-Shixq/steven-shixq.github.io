In recent years, more and more museums in China have been open to the public free of charge.

The number of museum exhibitions and visitors to museums has seen an obvious increase(increase significantly).

It has become very common to see that people stand in long queues in front of some some widely popular museums.

These museums must take measures to limit the number of visitors.

Nowadays, the forms of exhibitions become increasingly diverse.

Some large museums take advantage of advanced technologies such as virtual reality and multimedia to make their exhibitions more attractive.

Quite a few museums also hold on-line exhibitions where people can enjoy the sight of rare and precious exhibits.

However, the experience of viewing the exhibits on sit (on the spot) is still more appealing (more attractive) to most visitors.

排长队：stand in long queues

广受欢迎的：widely popular

采取措施：take measures

越来越多样化：increasingly diverse

先进的技术：advanced technologies

虚拟现实：virtual reality

多媒体：multimedia

更具吸引力：more attractive/appealing

不少，相当多：quite a few

观赏：enjoy the sight of

珍惜展品: rare and precious exhibits

现场：on site/on the spot

近年来，中国越来越多的博物馆免费向公众开放。博物馆展览次数和参观人数都明显增长。在一些广受欢迎的博物馆门前，排长队已很常见。这些博物馆必须采取措施限制参观人数。如今，展览形式越来越多样。一些大型博物馆利用多媒体和虚拟现实等先进技术，使展览更具吸引力。不少博物馆还举办在线展览，人们可在网上观赏珍稀展品。然而，现场观看展品的体验对大多数参观者还是更具吸引力。
